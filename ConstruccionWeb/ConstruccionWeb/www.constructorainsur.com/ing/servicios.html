PRELIMINARY STUDIES

With a focus on the realization of integral projects, in Constructora Insur we offer a wide range of processes for the development of the whole project and during the construction of the entire work, our experience and precision, allow us to carry out work of planimetry and altimetry, as well as Several preliminary studies of each work, that do not carry out:

- Obtaining polygonals.
- Calculation of surfaces.
- Leveling and building configuration.
- Obtaining levels of profanity.
- Obtaining volumes of earthwoks.
- Drainage design.
- Design of roads and courtyards.

These procedures make up the range of applications at the beginning of any job and are of vital importance in construction INSUR we perform with professionalism the engineering of soils, which by means of open wells and boreholes allow us to obtain the stratigraphy and properties of the soil, with the purpose of generating optimal conditions of viability, trust and planning for the design and calculation of any type of foundation, guaranteeing reliable and safe constructions.
